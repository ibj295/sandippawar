About

Analytics work that starts messy and ships clean.

I work across the Microsoft Fabric and Power BI stack — building pipelines that take duplicate-heavy, inconsistent source data and turn it into governed, queryable semantic models. That means writing the PySpark that cleans bronze into silver, designing the bridge and dimension tables that make a star schema actually behave, and shaping Direct Lake reports that stay fast at scale.

Alongside the hands-on build work, I write and teach — breaking down Fabric and Power BI concepts for other analysts, from pipeline activity types to Direct Lake vs. Import vs. DirectQuery trade-offs.
